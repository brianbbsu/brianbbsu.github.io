自傳 Autobiography

我是蘇柏瑄，於西元2001年生。 我喜愛一切和資訊相關的事物，享受自己探索這塊新奇的領域，更追求著解決問題時的成就感。 我約莫在國小四年級時接觸到程式，在網路、家人與同儕的協助、砥礪下，我經歷過了世界機器人奧林匹克、 台灣國際科展與國際資訊奧林匹亞等大型賽事，也接觸到了資訊領域中不同的面向。 不論是現在、過去還是將來，這將一直是一趟奇幻之旅，有著許多未知事物等待著我去探索。 還有，最重要的，永遠保有熱情與快樂。

I'm Brian Su from Taiwan, born in 2001. I love every Computer Science related stuff. I enjoy exploring this fascinating realm piece by piece, and desire the sense of achievement gained when solving a problem. I started programming since grade 4. With help from my parents, schoolmates, and the Internet, I exprienced many grand tournaments. Including World Robot Olympiad, Taiwan International Science Fair, and Internationl Olympiad in Informatics. I learned different aspects of CS from these expriences. No matter when, this has been, and will be a wonderful advanture, with many unknowns waiting for me to explore. And, most importantly, always have enthusiasm and happiness.
