Autobiography

I'm Brian Su from Taiwan, born in 2001. I’m a gold medalist in Olympiad in Informatics. I love programming, I love everything about Computer Science. I started programming when I was 10. With the help from my parents, peers, and the Internet, I participated in some international tournaments, such as International Olympiad in Informatics, World Robot Olympiad, and Taiwan International Science Fair. From these experiences, I learned different aspects of computer science. I hunger for exploring the fascinating journey pieces by piece, and I'm addict to the sense of achievement given by solving problems. Always and forever, it is an amazing and astonishing adventure with many unknowns waiting for me to embark on. What's most important, always have enthusiasm and happiness.
